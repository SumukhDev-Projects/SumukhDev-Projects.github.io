About
From torque specs to transformer models.

I'm a Data Analyst with a Master's in Engineering Management from UIUC, where I focused on data analytics. My work lives at the intersection of data engineering and applied AI — building the kind of infrastructure that actually gets used. At UIUC Extension, I own the pipeline serving public health data for 17 state health departments, with outputs feeding directly into USDA funding decisions.

Outside the day job, I build LLM-powered tools end-to-end: a document extraction API with per-field confidence scoring, a natural language-to-SQL interface with two-layer read-only enforcement, a data quality agent that auto-generates dbt tests, and an autonomous analytics agent built on LangGraph. My Mechanical Engineering foundation means I think in systems — less interested in whether a dashboard looks right than in what breaks upstream to make the number wrong.
